Gallerie Hoffmann

For more than 8 years I worked at Galerie Hoffmann. Galerie Hoffmann is known for concrete and constructive art. It has a history of more than 50 years documenting and exhibiting this art movement and the interantional artists it is representing. I was helping exhibiting and selling art at both of the gallery locations at the Görbelheimer Mühle and Ossenheim in Friedberg (Hessen, Germany) in Friedberg (Hessen, Germany). My focus was on taking photos of artworks and exhibitions, I also developed a PHP script connecting the gallery's inventory system with the website. I learned a lot about art and the art market and had the chance to work together with many great artists from all over the world.
