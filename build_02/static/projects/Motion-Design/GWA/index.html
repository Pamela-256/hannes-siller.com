This video was made in a team with Matthias Zosel, he created the entering shot (Adobe After Effects) and Arne Dikomey, who made the flying comet (Autodesk Maya), I did the final logo and typography animations (Autodesk Maya). It has been shown as the opening trailer at the GWA Junior Agency award at the Media Campus "Unter den Eichen" in Wiesbaden. Parts of the design have been adapted to the overall design of the event. The event has been organized by the GWA (German Association of Communications Agencies) and students of the RheinMain University of Applied Sciences and Arts. (supervision by Prof. Rüdiger Pichler).
