This showreel is containing some projects I worked on when I studied visual communication at RheinMain University of Applied Sciences and Arts. The projects include also some early commercial projects I made when I worked for the Arts Others agency in Bad Homburg. The Musicvideo was a team effort together with Matthias Zosel My focus was on 3D animation and motion design. The software I used for these projects include Autodesk Maya, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe Photoshop.
