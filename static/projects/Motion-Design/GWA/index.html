I made this video in a team with Matthias Zosel and Arne Dikomey. It has been shown as the opening trailer at the GWA Junior Agency award at the Media Campus "Unter den Eichen" in Wiesbaden. Parts of the design have been adapted to the overall design of the event. The event has been organized by the GWA (German Association of Communications Agencies) and students of the University of Applied Sciences Wiesbaden. (supervision by Prof. Rüdiger Pichler).
