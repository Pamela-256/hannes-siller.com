This opening trailer was produced for the held at the Media Campus "Unter den Eichen" in Wiesbaden. The project was a collaboration with Matthias Zosel, who created the entering shot (Adobe After Effects) and Arne Dikomey, who modeled and animated the flying comet (Autodesk Maya), I was responsible for the final 3D logo and typography animations using Autodesk Maya.
Beyond the trailer, elements of our motion design were adapted for the overall visual identity of the event. The ceremony was organized by the GWA in partnership with students from the RheinMain sity of Applied Sciences and Arts, under supervision of Prof. Rüdiger Pichler.
