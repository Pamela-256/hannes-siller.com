This showreel contains a selection of projects from my time studying Visual Communication at RheinMain University of Applied Sciences and Arts. It also features early commercial work from my time at the Arts Others agency in Bad Homburg. The music video included was a collaborative effort with Matthias Zosel Throughout these projects, my primary focus was on 3D animation and motion design. The software I used include Autodesk Maya, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe Photoshop.
