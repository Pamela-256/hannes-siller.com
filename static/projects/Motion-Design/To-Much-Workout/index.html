This animation was created for the exhibition "Workout," which marked the beginning of my involvement with the art collective Projektraum 61. The project was part of "Werkwoche"—a collaborative art residency where we work together for one week, culminating in an exhibition of the results. The event is held in a gallery within a historic barn operated by wetterausicht.de, a non-profit art and cultural association where I have volunteered for many years. Since its inception in 2016, we have continued this annual tradition for nine consecutive years by the time of writing. Eventually, I moved into the house to which the barn belongs, living there with artist friends and establishing a studio space to focus on my subsequent art projects.
