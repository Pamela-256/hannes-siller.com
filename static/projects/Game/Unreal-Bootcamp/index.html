The Epic Bootcamp Animation 2023 was a intensive four-week course created by Epic Games. It was hosted by UT-Hub and instructors form INCAS Training and other Epic training partners. Hundreds of Students participated worldwide. The focus was on animated films using Unreal Engine 5.

I had to support my girlfriend who was working in brazil at the time and so I had to move to brazil for three month just before the course started. This cicumstance made me to get up at 3 am to attend the live sessions (which were in the central european time zone). So for me it was a intense but also very rewarding experience. I bought the main character and the environment at the Unreal Marketplace. Special Thanks to Tom Baumann how was so kind to host a little motion capturing session in his studio in switzerland (quantum-stage.com). It was amazing to be able to direct a short motion capturing recording (via video call) with the great acting of Ophir Raray.
