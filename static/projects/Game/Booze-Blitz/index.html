This lillte prototype was a reult of the Midsummer game jam at Spelkollektivet in Sweden and we got the 3rd prize for best game mechanic. It was a in person game jam with over 60 people participating the whole day for one week. It was part of my 3 month stay at the Spelkollektivet. Where I lived and shared office spaces together with a every week fluctuating mix of 50 to 70 game developers from all over the world.
Some stay there for a few days and some for years.
There is a little game conference every week where everybody who wants (and stays at the house for the time) is pressenting his or her acctual progress. You can view it usually every thursday on Spelkollektivet on Twitch.
I can recommend this place for everybody who is interested in game development, excpcially for indi gamedevelopment.

The prgramming is done by Antonio Ackalin and Michael Campos, the art is done by Axel Magnuson and me. Gamedesign and Concept was a effort of all of us.
