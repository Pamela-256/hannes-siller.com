Robodraw

The robots are battery-operated, autonomously moving vehicles, each measuring 22x15x15 cm. They are equipped with ultrasonic sensors that allow them to perceive distances. <BR> The robots operate based on simple predefined rules: 'Move forward until an obstacle appears, then try evade it at a defined angle in the direction where the obstacle can be bypassed most quickly.' The robots move on a 80 x 120 cm sheet of paper (approximately equivalent to DIN A0). The paper is placed on a table with 20 cm-high walls along its edges, allowing the robots to recognize the boundaries of their operating area.
